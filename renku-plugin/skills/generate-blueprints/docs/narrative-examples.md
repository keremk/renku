# Narrative Examples

**Example 1**
Inquiry Prompt: Tell me about battle of Waterloo
Narrative:
- This is a setup for creating a documentary video about the history.
- Will require a search about the history and summarizing it into a narrative text that fits into the time limit. 
- Will involve a background narration audio
- Individual segments will feature 2 or 3 images with Kenn Burns effects
- There will be some images needed to show the historical maps
- Depending on the length, there might be a few sections, where a historian narrating with a video some key highlights. These will be indispersed into the overall narrative.
- There will be a background music that is suitable to the imagery.

**Example 2**
Inquiry Prompt: Create a fairy tale story about a kid who wondered in the forest and had an adventure
Narrative:
- This will need to create an interesting story with story arclines. See ./story-arclines.md for a set of those arclines. Based on picking one, there should be a script to follow.
- A background narration audio that sets the story and also interjects throughout the story.
- Character dialogs based on the story generated.
- Individual segments will be a combination of narrative scene setting, character dialogs, actions etc. So video segments are preferred.
- The segments should have continuity with start and end images to ensure that or some continues cuts.
- There should be some character consistency and they need to be generated and used throughout as reference images.

**Example 3**
Inquiry Prompt: I want to have an ad featuring the new board game intended for families
Narrative:
- A narrative script for the ad.
- This needs a hero character or two who will be featured and consistent. So initial character generation.
- Multiple shots of the product to be highlighted, either provided by user or generated.
- Video segments that follow the narrative, the hero character and the product placement.
- Likely some background music.
- Ends with a catch line and call to action (generated as an image)

**Example 4** 
Inquiry Prompt: I want this person to interview famous actors in their famous moviesets.
Narrative:
- Identify (or use the provided) actors and search for scenes that would be most familiar to users
- Requires the consistent character doing the narration.
- Each segment puts the person and the actor (character) in the familiar scene.
- Scene to scene video transitions needs to be controlled and seamless. 
- Background music to accompany the narrative.

**Example 5**
Inquiry Prompt: I want a home renovation video that takes this image of a run-down place and turns it into a modern home office.
Narrative:
- A narrative about how to repair, renovate and build the room in a step by step fashion.
- Some catchy background music.
- One or two 10 second or so video segments, that continuously builds up the based on the narrative.
