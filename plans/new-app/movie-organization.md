Subject:

> **Preparation of the Siege of Constantinople, 1451–April 1453**

Important framing: this is **not yet the siege itself**. The actual siege began in April 1453 and ended with the city’s fall on **May 29, 1453**. The preparation phase includes Mehmed II’s strategic, diplomatic, engineering, logistical, naval, and psychological setup before the walls were attacked. Britannica specifically notes Rumelihisarı, Urban’s cannon, transport of cannon from Edirne, and the assembly of Ottoman forces outside Constantinople by April 1453. ([Encyclopedia Britannica][1])

Better structure:

> One sequence about logistics.

> Each clip has one beat.
